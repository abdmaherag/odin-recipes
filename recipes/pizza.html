Pizza

This is an italian dish; Pizza

This classic pizza recipe is a delicious symphony of flavors. with a golden crust, tangy sauce, creamy mozzarella, and your favorite toppings, it is a perfect blend of simplicity and satisfaction.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • toppis of your choice

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven (recommended temperature around 450F or 230C)
  2. roll out the pizza dough
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough onto a lightly greased pizza pna or baking sheet
  4. Spead the pizza sauce evenly over the dough
  5. sprinkle mozzarella on it
  6. add your toppings
  7. Bake the piuzza for about 12-15 minutes
  8. Remove the pizza from the oven and wait a few minutes before slicing
  9. Enjoy your hot delicious homemade pizza!
